the design process for this website

I based the design of my portfolio website based on the following two reasoning:

  • At the moment, I consider myself a layman with respect to web development, and as implied in my search for entry-level position, I am capable of intermediate level of web development as of now. On that basis, I chose to keep the design simple and minimalistic, demonstrating what I am capable of at the moment than to over-extend beyond my abilities. Additionally, in terms of demonstrating what I can do and my strength at the moment, I decided to heavily rely on Bootstrap to develop a responsive website.
  • Considering that the intended audience, or user persona, of portfolio websites will most likely be individuals sifting through numerous job applications and portfolio sites, simple design with few distractions seemed optimal for user interaction of target audience.
